About us

We turn loops of yarn into little companions.

Honey Loop Bears is a small studio inspired by childhood keepsakes, cozy nurseries, and the joy of giving something truly handmade. Every teddy begins as a skein of soft yarn and is shaped stitch by stitch with careful tension, secure seams, and expressive embroidery.

We believe handmade toys should feel personal, durable, and full of charm. Whether you choose a ready-made bear or request a custom color palette, we craft each piece as if it were going to someone we love.

  • Safety eyes or fully embroidered faces available
  • Custom colors for baby showers, birthdays, and keepsake gifts
  • Care instructions included with every order
